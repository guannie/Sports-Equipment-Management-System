ABOUT

ORGANIZATION’S BACKGROUND

We decided to develop Sports Equipment Management system at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Taman Universiti. SMK Taman Universiti is located 6 kilometres from Universiti Teknologi Malaysia. It tooks about 15 minutes to go there. The organization already have a system but the data is unorganized. The current system combine sports equipment with other equipment such as laboratory equipment.

Problem Definition

Sports Equipment Management System is a product that manage any type of sports equipment such as football, hockey stick and more. The management process includes adding new data for sports equipment, removing data for sports equipment, modifying the data for sports equipment, the usage of sports equipment and more. The client requires the product because the system will reduce the time spent on managing the sports equipment. The client is running out of time as there are meetings and classes to be attended sooner or later. Other than that, the new system will also introduce a few new features to further reduce the workload of the client. For instance, a report generator.

About The New System

There is previously no proper Sports Equipment Management System in the client’s organization. All the records for the sports equipment are stored in paper-written format and some are computed using Microsoft Excel. Below are the list of the new/updated system.

  • The client can insert new equipment info by choosing the insert tab

  • The client can update the sports equipment information that is already in the database if an update is needed

  • The client can also delete the sports equipment information that is already in the database

  • The client can also choose whether to lend or return the sports equipment by completing the form under the lending/returning tab
